Community Farmers Markets | Mapping Around Data

The Problem

Community Farmers Markets would like to harness their data to see trends across years and MARTA stations. They would also like to use data to make decisions about where and how to expand. In order to aid them, data will need to be displayed in one easily-accessed location.

Background

  • The main software Community Farmers Markets (CFM) is using is Square for their transactional needs
  • Google Maps is a common resource for mapping needs, and we are able to gather demographic heat map data from existing Tableau visualizations
  • CFM Market Data is available to us at any time
